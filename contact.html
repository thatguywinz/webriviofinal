(02) Frequently asked

Questions
we hear often.

Do you work with businesses outside the US?

Yes. We work with English-speaking businesses across the US, Canada, UK, and Australia. Time-zone overlap is usually easy — we block early or late hours as needed for calls.

Can you write the website copy for us?

Copywriting is included in the Flagship+ package, or available as an add-on to any other package. If you prefer to write your own, we'll guide it into shape during the build and tune headlines, CTAs, and section flow as we go.

Will I fully own the website after launch?

Completely. You own the code, the domain, and every account we set up. You can walk away from our hosting anytime with no penalty and migrate elsewhere — we'll even help with the export.

What if I only need a redesign, not a full rebuild?

A redesign is a great fit for our Foundation package. Tell us more on a 15-minute fit call and we'll scope it honestly — sometimes the answer is "keep your current site, we'll fix the two pages that matter."

How long does a project take from start to launch?

Approximately four weeks from kickoff to launch for a Flagship build. A Foundation redesign often ships in under three weeks. We give you a week-by-week timeline on day one and stick to it.

How much does a Webrivio website cost?

Pricing depends on the scope and complexity of your project. Send us an inquiry and we'll follow up with a custom quote — usually within one business day.
