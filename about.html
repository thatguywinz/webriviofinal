(01) Why Webrivio exists

Small businesses were overcharged.
So we published the price.

We are two people from Toronto who got tired of watching small businesses pay agency prices for template websites.

We entered a competition and won. That led to an interview, and then a place in Summer Company — a Province of Ontario program for young founders — which came with a $3,000 grant. That grant is what started the studio.

We applied because we kept seeing the same story. An owner hands a designer thousands of dollars, waits months, and gets back something generic with their logo dropped on it. Slow, expensive, and not built to bring in work.

So we did the opposite. Our price is published instead of hidden behind a quote: from $499, flat and scope-dependent. And the build is fast — live in 48 hours once your content and payment are in. The price is low on purpose. The work is not.
