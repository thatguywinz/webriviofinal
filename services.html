(02) Pricing — published, not quoted in a back room

Web design pricing for Toronto small businesses.

Starting price · CAD
Websites from $499flat

Not a teaser rate. $499 buys a real, custom-built site — designed for your business, coded by hand, live in 48 hours once your content and payment are in. One number, agreed before we start, and it doesn't move unless you ask for more.

What moves the price

01

How many pages

$499 covers a focused build — the handful of pages a small business needs to get found and get called. A deep services section, a page per GTA suburb you serve, or a blog all add real design and build work, so they add to the quote.

02

Who writes the words

Hand us finished copy and you pay less. Ask us to write it — headlines, service descriptions, the whole voice — and that's a writer's hours on top of a designer's. We'd rather charge for it honestly than hide it in the base price.

03

Where the images come from

Your own photos cost nothing. Licensed stock costs a little. A photographer on your job site in Toronto costs what a photographer costs — and it's usually the single biggest upgrade a trades or hospitality site can make.

04

What it has to plug into

A checkout, a live booking calendar, a payment processor, a CRM handoff, a members' area. Each one is a system to build, wire up and test, not a checkbox — and each one is priced as its own line, visible to you.

05

Whether we look after it afterwards

Care is optional and month-to-month: hosting, SSL, backups, monitoring, and content edits. Skip it and we hand over the files and the domain. There is no penalty for walking away, because it was never our website.

No retainer you didn't ask for. No platform fee. No line item we can't explain back to you in one sentence. That is the entire reason this studio exists.
