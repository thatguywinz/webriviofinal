Process · How it goes

From outreach.
To delivery.

No drawn-out sales call. We start with focused outreach, show the site direction in a 15-20 minute demo, then move into payment, seven days of polish and setup, and a clean handoff.

Timeline after payment7 days
Your time investment15-20 min demo
Meetings1 short demo
Delivery optionsHosted or source
01

Outreach

Before the demo

We start with focused outreach and a quick fit check. If the opportunity is real, we book a short demo instead of asking you to sit through a long discovery call.

01
Initial outreach

We identify where the current website is holding the business back and what a stronger version should accomplish.

02
Fit check

We confirm the basics: business type, service area, goals, timeline, and whether our offer makes sense.

03
Demo scheduled

If there is a clear path forward, we set a 15-20 minute demo so you can see the direction before committing.

02

Demo & Payment

15-20 minutes

The demo is direct: what we noticed, what we would improve, what the finished site will include, and how delivery works. If it feels right, payment starts the project.

01
Demo walkthrough

We show the proposed direction, page structure, core message, and what the final site is built to do.

02
Scope confirmation

We confirm pages, content needs, hosting preference, and anything required before the 7-day window begins.

03
Payment

If you approve the demo, payment locks the work and moves the site into final polish and setup.

03

Polish & Setup

7 days after payment

Once payment is in, we spend seven days tightening the website, preparing the hosting path, and checking the details that make the handoff smooth.

01
Website polish

Headlines, calls to action, spacing, content flow, and mobile presentation are tuned before delivery.

02
Hosting setup

If you want Webrivio hosting, we prepare the managed hosting environment, SSL, forms, and analytics.

03
Final QA

We test the site across devices, check forms and links, and make sure the delivered files are clean.

04

Delivery

Final handoff

The finished website is delivered with a clear ownership path. You can keep hosting with us month to month, or take the source code and run it wherever you prefer.

01
Managed hosting

If you want Webrivio to host it, the site stays on a monthly recurring hosting plan with ongoing technical care.

02
Source handoff

If you do not need our hosting, we deliver the GitHub repo or source code so you can host it independently.

03
Ownership

You receive the site files and access details tied to the option you choose. The website is yours.

(05) What we need from you

Less than an hour of your time,
with a clear 7-day window.

—01

A 15-20 min demo

We show the proposed direction, answer practical questions, and confirm whether the site is worth moving forward.

—02

A clear yes or no

If the demo makes sense, payment starts the 7-day polishing and setup window. If not, no hard feelings.

—03

Fast final notes

During polish, send any must-have changes to copy, details, contact info, services, or calls to action.

—04

Your hosting choice

Choose monthly Webrivio hosting, or take the GitHub repo/source code and host the website wherever you prefer.
