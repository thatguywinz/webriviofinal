(01) The 48-hour process

Web design in Toronto, start to finish, in 48 hours.

Four steps. Only one of them is work for you, and it takes about an hour. Everything else happens on our side, on a clock you can hold us to.

The one condition The 48 hours begin the moment your content and your payment are both in — and not a minute before. We put that in writing on every page it appears, because a deadline with a hidden start time is not a deadline. If your photos are still coming, the clock is still waiting.

01

The fit call

15-20 minutes · Free

A short call — not a discovery process, not a sales sequence. You get a straight answer on whether we are the right fit before you spend anything.

01

What you tell us

The business, where you work — Toronto, the GTA, a few postcodes — and what the site actually has to do: calls, bookings, quote requests.

02

What we show you

The direction we would take, the pages you genuinely need, and exactly what is included at the flat price. From $499, said out loud on the call.

03

No pressure

If it is not a fit, we say so on the call. There is no proposal to chase and nothing to unsubscribe from afterwards.

02

Content & payment

Starts the 48-hour clock

This is the only step that lands on you. Two things arrive from your side, and the moment the second one does, the 48 hours are running.

01

Your content

Logo, photos, services, hours, service area, and anything you want said in your own words. Whatever you already have is usually enough — we work with it.

02

Your payment

Flat, from $499, scope-dependent — the number we agreed on the call. Nothing is metered and nothing is added later.

03

The clock starts

48 hours, counted from the moment both content and payment are in. If one of them is still coming, the clock has not started — and we will tell you that rather than quietly run it down.

03

Design & build

The 48 hours

Design, copy and code in one pass, by the person who took your call. No handoffs between departments, because there are no departments.

01

Designed for your market

A custom layout built around the one action that matters to your business — not a template with your logo dropped into the corner.

02

Built and tested

Hand-coded, fast, and checked on real phones before anyone else sees it. Forms, links and contact details all get tested, not assumed.

03

You see it live

A real URL you can open, not a slide deck of screenshots. You look at it, you send your notes, we fix them.

04

Handoff

Yours to keep

The site is live and the code is yours. Nothing about the handoff is designed to keep you here — staying should be a choice, not a trap.

01

You own the code

Source files and the repository, handed to you. No proprietary builder, no page-builder licence, no ransom on your own website.

02

Hosting, if you want it

Optional managed hosting with ongoing technical care — SSL, forms, analytics, updates. Take it or leave it; the site works either way.

03

Or host it yourself

Prefer your own setup? Take the repo and run it wherever you like. We hand over the access details and get out of the way.
